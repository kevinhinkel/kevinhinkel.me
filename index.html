Design

Design is clear communication through words and pictures. It is interwoven into the tapestry of every day life and gives us certain distinction for how we communicate visually. Without interesting color, intriguing type and great pictures the world would be a different place. Whether viewing design with a critical eye or by casually observing it, the onlooker is influenced. Quality versus quantity is a concept I appreciate. Balancing these concepts is a philosophical way to find success in design. Out of all of the experiences that come with creating an art form, sharing it with others is my most favorite. Feel free to peruse my site at your leisure and drop me a line with any questions or imput at the email listed above. Thank you for stopping by!
