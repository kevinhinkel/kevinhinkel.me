Autobiography

I am a well-rounded designer with a passion for creating aesthetically pleasing and exciting design. My professional experience consists of graphic design, art production, publishing, advertising and branding. I’ve honed in a variety of other media skill sets and am always trying to learn more web development.

I grew up making my own holiday cards instead of buying Hallmark cards. I was the project coordinator for the group projects that took place for any visual assignment in school. In college I majored in art and minored in graphic design and worked for the school's publishing company in photo touchup and color correction.

One of my favorite jobs was working for Designwise Art in Portland, OR. I was able to work with a wide range of media. I worked doing layout, Photoshop art, package design, photography and logo design. My ideal work environments are ones that are calm but busy, have vision, good teamwork, talent, sense of humor, great culture and people are able to be themselves.
